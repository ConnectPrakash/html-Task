Departments

BCA

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate degree program in the field of computer applications. It is a popular choice among students who have an interest in computer science and wish to pursue a career in the IT industry


Duration: Typically, the BCA course is of three years duration, divided into six semesters. However, the duration may vary depending on the educational institution and the country.

B.COM CA

The B.Com (CA) course, also known as Bachelor of Commerce with Computer Applications, is an undergraduate degree program that integrates commerce with computer applications.It combines elements of traditional commerce studies with computer-related subjects to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of both fields.


Duration: Similar to other undergraduate programs, the B.Com (CA) course is typically of three years duration, divided into six semesters. However, the duration may vary depending on the educational institution and the country.

B.SC COMPUTER SCIENCE

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (B.Sc Computer Science) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the theoretical and practical aspects of computer science. It equips students with the knowledge and skills required to design, develop, and analyze software systems, algorithms, and computing technologies.


Duration: The B.Sc Computer Science course typically spans over three years, divided into six semesters. However, the duration may vary depending on the educational institution and the country.
